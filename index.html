Our Story

Built on trust, run on care.

SRRA Multispeciality Hospital began with a simple premise: quality healthcare shouldn't be a privilege reserved for the few. Today, we serve families across Thoothukudi with the same conviction we started with.

Mission

Deliver accurate, affordable, and timely medical care to every patient who walks through our doors — day or night.

Vision

Become the most trusted multispeciality hospital in the region, known equally for clinical excellence and human warmth.

Core Values

Integrity in diagnosis, transparency in cost, and dignity in every patient interaction — no exceptions.

Why Choose SRRA?

  • Round-the-clock emergency and ICU readiness
  • Specialists across ten core departments under one roof
  • Transparent, affordable billing with no hidden costs

Departments

Specialities under one roof.

Ten dedicated departments, coordinated as a single care team around every patient.

Cardiology

Comprehensive heart care — from ECG and stress testing to interventional cardiology.

General Medicine

Everyday illness, chronic condition management, and preventive health screening.

Orthopaedics

Bone, joint, and spine care — from fractures to full joint replacement.

Neurology

Diagnosis and treatment of brain, spine, and nervous system disorders.

Diabetology

Personalised blood sugar management and long-term diabetes care plans.

Emergency Care

24×7 trauma and emergency response with a dedicated rapid-response team.

ICU

Critical care monitoring with advanced life-support and round-the-clock nursing.

Pediatrics

Gentle, specialised care for infants, children, and adolescents.

Gynecology

Women's health, prenatal care, and delivery services with a dedicated maternity wing.

General Surgery

Precision surgical care across a broad range of routine and complex procedures.
